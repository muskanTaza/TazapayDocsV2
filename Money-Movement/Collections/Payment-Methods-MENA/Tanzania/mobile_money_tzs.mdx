Mobile money in Tanzania is a mobile phone-based platform providing a wide array of financial services to a significant portion of the Tanzanian population.

# How it Works?

* Users register for mobile money services with their mobile phone numbers through providers like M-Pesa, Airtel Money, and Tigo Pesa.
* They can deposit and withdraw cash at a widespread network of mobile money agents.
* The system enables instant sending and receiving of money between users.
* It facilitates various transactions such as bill payments, airtime purchases, and access to other financial products like savings, loans, and insurance.

# Payment Method type

Users can use the below payment method type to create a transaction using mobile money in the available geography:

* Tanzania: `mobile_money_tzs`

# Limits and Expiration

The following is the upper and lower limits for Mobile Money transactions:

* **Minimum Limit**: USD 0.5
* **Maximum Limit**: USD 1929

The default expiration for a transaction is **5 Days.**
