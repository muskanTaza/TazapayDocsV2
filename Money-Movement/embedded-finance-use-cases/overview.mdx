Tazapay enables platforms and global businesses to embed cross-border money movement, treasury, and payouts directly into their products. This section highlights common embedded finance use cases and how customers use Tazapay to power them.

These use cases are especially relevant for:

- **Global employers** managing distributed teams across borders
- **Fintech platforms** building payment experiences
- **Neobanks and wallets** offering international money movement
- **Stablecoin-native businesses** requiring fiat on/off ramps
