title: "Onboarding Requirements for Businesses"
---
## Required Information

### 1. Business Details

- **Registered Business Name**
- **Business Registration / Incorporation Certificate**
- **Registered Address and Operational Address**  
  - Documentary proof required (e.g. registration documents, utility bills)
- **Nature of Business**  
  - Description of products and/or services offered
- **Shareholding Structure**
- **Official Website**
- **Monthly Turnover**

### 2. Details of Key Individuals

Details are required for **Ultimate Beneficial Owners (UBOs)**, **Directors**, and **Authorised Representatives**:

- **Contact Information**
- **Proof of Identity**  
  - Passport, National ID, or Residence Permit
- **Proof of Address**  
  - Bank statement, utility bill, or equivalent document
- **Board Resolution (if applicable)**  
  - Required if the authorised representative is **not a Director**, confirming they are authorised to open and operate an account with Tazapay

## Additional Requirements

The above list represents general onboarding requirements.  
Depending on factors such as:

- industry
- business model
- Risk and compliance assessments

Tazapay may request additional information or documentation to complete the onboarding process.
