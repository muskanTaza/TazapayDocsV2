A popular online payment method in Italy that allows customers to make secure and immediate bank transfers directly from their online banking accounts.

# How It Works

* During checkout, the customer selects "MyBank" as their payment method.
* They are then redirected to their online banking environment within the secure MyBank platform.
* Using their existing online banking credentials, they authenticate the payment.
* Funds are transferred directly from their account to the merchant in real-time.

# Payment Method type

Users can use the below payment method type to create a transaction using MyBank in the available geography

* Italy: `mybank_eur`

# Limits and Expiration

The following is the upper and lower limits for MyBank transactions:

* **Minimum Limit**: USD 1
* **Maximum Limit**: USD 15000

The default expiration for a transaction is **30 minutes.**
