---
title: "Onboarding Requirements for Individuals"
---

## Required Information

- **Full Name**
- **Date of Birth**
- **Nationality**
- **Residential Address**
- **Proof of Identity**  
  - Passport, National ID, or Residence Permit
- **Proof of Address**  
  - Utility bill, bank statement, or other valid address document

## Additional Requirements

In certain cases, based on regulatory obligations or risk assessments, we may request additional information or documentation to complete the onboarding process.
