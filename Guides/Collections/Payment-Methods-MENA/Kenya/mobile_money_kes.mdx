Mobile money in Kenya is a mobile phone-based platform providing a wide array of financial services to a significant portion of the Tanzanian population.

# How it Works?

* Users register for mobile money services with their mobile phone numbers.
* They can deposit and withdraw cash through a vast network of mobile money agents.
* Send and receive money to other users instantly.
* Pay bills, purchase airtime, and even access micro-loans and savings accounts.

# Payment Method type

Users can use the below payment method type to create a transaction using mobile money in the available geography:

* Kenya: `mobile_money_kes`

# Limits and Expiration

The following is the upper and lower limits for Mobile Money transactions:

* **Minimum Limit**: USD 0.5
* **Maximum Limit**: USD 1098

The default expiration for a transaction is **5 Days.**
