A widely adopted digital payment platform and e-wallet that has become a significant player in Indonesia's burgeoning fintech landscape.

# How It Works

* Users download the OVO application and register using their mobile phone number.
* They can top up their OVO balance through various methods, including bank transfers, ATM, debit cards, credit cards, and at a vast network of OVO Booths located in shopping malls and retail outlets.
* Once funded, users can make payments for a wide range of services both online and offline. This includes payments for Grab services (ride-hailing, food delivery), at participating merchants (using QR codes or the OVO app), for bill payments, mobile top-ups, and even for investments through integration with other platforms.
* OVO also features peer-to-peer transfers to other OVO users.

# Payment Method type

Users can use the below payment method type to create a transaction using OVO in the available geography

# Limits and Expiration

The following is the upper and lower limits for OVO transactions:

* **Minimum Limit**: USD 0.06
* **Maximum Limit**: USD 1200

The default expiration for a transaction is **70 seconds.**
