You can configure your deposit address from the Tazapay dashboard. Please note that this configuration can only be performed once before any transactions are associated with the deposit address.

## How to Configure a Deposit Address

You can configure your deposit address directly from the Tazapay Dashboard.

### Steps

1. Go to **[Home](https://dashboard.tazapay.com/home) â†’ Settings**
2. Navigate to **Deposit Address**
3. Click **Edit**
4. Enter your preferred deposit address details
5. Click **Update** to save
