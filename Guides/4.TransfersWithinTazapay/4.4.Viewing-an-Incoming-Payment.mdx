title: "Viewing an Incoming Payment"
---

# **1. Using the dashboard**

Each incoming payment is represented by a collect object on Tazapay. Refer [<u>here</u>](https://docs.tazapay.com/docs/viewing-collect-incoming-payments) to access how to view collects.

# **2. Using API**

You will receive a collect.succeeded webhook on your configured endpoint for a successful transaction.

Related Link: [<u>Collect Webhooks</u>](https://docs.tazapay.com/docs/collect)
