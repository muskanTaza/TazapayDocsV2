**Entities** are **individuals or businesses** that a platform can onboard on Tazapay to **create collections and payouts on their behalf**.

Entities do not integrate directly with Tazapay. Instead, they are **linked to the platformâ€™s primary Tazapay account**, allowing the platform to facilitate payments **on behalf of these entities** while retaining centralized control.

## Why is this required ?

1. Regulatory Transparency
2. Strong Compliance & Risk Controls
3. Clear Fund Traceability
4. Enables Marketplace & Platform Use Cases
5. Bank & Partner Acceptance

## How  to create entities ?

Entity is not a generally available feature, please contact [support@tazapay.com](mailto:support@tazapay.com) to enable this feature if not enabled on your account. But if we discover that your usecase mandatorly requires you to create transactions on behalf of entities (For example - You work with nested flows), then we will be mandating entity creation alongside a transaction for your account.

 Entities can be created both via the dashboard and the API.

Refer entity API to create and submit entities .

### Dashboard

1. Once entities is enabled for you, you should be able to see an entities tab in the left Nav Bar.

![Screenshot 2025 12 28 At 11 58 15 PM](/images/Screenshot2025-12-28at11.58.15PM.png)

2. You can click on that to move to the entities section where you can view th list of entities under you account and also create new entities.

![Screenshot 2025 12 29 At 12 00 57 AM](/images/Screenshot2025-12-29at12.00.57AM.png)

3. You can also find the Create Entity CTA on the Create payout screen , you can use that to create a payout on behalf of a entity.

![Screenshot 2025 12 29 At 12 01 24 AM](/images/Screenshot2025-12-29at12.01.24AM.png)

Refer to Approval Lifecyle to understand how entities are approved and if and when an approval would be necessary to create a payout or collect.
