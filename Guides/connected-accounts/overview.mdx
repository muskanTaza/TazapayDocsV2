Connected Accounts allow platforms, marketplaces, and fintechs to onboard and manage their own customers directly on Tazapay, while remaining completely white-labelled.

In this model:

- Your customers can be businesses or individuals
- Tazapay gains the required visibility into underlying customers to meet regulatory and licensing obligations
- The onboarding experience is silent, meaning your customers do not see or interact with Tazapay directly
- Funds movement, compliance checks, FX, and settlement are handled securely by Tazapay in the background
