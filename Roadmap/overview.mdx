# Roadmap

Explore what we're building and what's coming next for Tazapay. Our roadmap is organized by quarter and represents our current plans, which may be adjusted based on customer feedback and market needs.

---

## Q1 2026 (January - March)

### In Development

**Mass Payout Scheduling**
- Schedule recurring payouts for payroll and vendor payments
- CSV bulk upload for processing thousands of payouts
- Automated retry logic for failed transactions
- Custom scheduling rules (weekly, bi-weekly, monthly)

**Enhanced Compliance Suite**
- Automated sanctions screening for all transactions
- Enhanced PEP (Politically Exposed Person) checks
- Country-specific compliance rule engine
- Audit trail and reporting dashboard

**Mobile App (Beta)**
- iOS and Android native applications
- Full payout and collection management on mobile
- Biometric authentication (Face ID, Touch ID)
- Push notifications for transaction updates
- QR code scanning for virtual account payments

---

## Q2 2026 (April - June)

### Planned

**White-label Solution**
- Fully customizable branding for platform partners
- Custom domain support
- Branded email notifications
- Embedded payment widgets
- Custom workflow configurations

**Advanced Analytics Dashboard**
- Real-time transaction monitoring and insights
- Custom report builder with 50+ metrics
- FX exposure analysis and hedging recommendations
- Cash flow forecasting and projections
- Export to BI tools (Tableau, Power BI integration)

**Crypto Payout Expansion**
- Support for 10+ additional cryptocurrencies
- DeFi integration for yield generation
- Automated crypto-to-fiat conversion
- Multi-signature wallet support
- Hardware wallet integration

---

## Q3 2026 (July - September)

### Under Consideration

**AI-Powered Fraud Detection**
- Machine learning-based transaction risk scoring
- Automated suspicious activity alerts
- Pattern recognition for fraud prevention
- Real-time transaction blocking for high-risk payments
- False positive reduction through adaptive learning

**Invoice Management System**
- Generate and send invoices directly from Tazapay
- Automatic payment reminders
- Multi-currency invoicing
- Payment link generation
- Invoice reconciliation with payouts

**API V4 Launch**
- GraphQL support alongside REST
- Improved rate limits and performance
- Enhanced webhook customization
- Real-time event streaming via WebSockets
- Better error handling and validation

---

## Q4 2026 (October - December)

### Exploring

**Embedded Finance Toolkit**
- Pre-built UI components for faster integration
- No-code payout workflow builder
- Sandbox environment improvements
- Developer playground with live testing
- SDK support for Python, Java, Ruby

**Cross-Border Cards Program**
- Virtual and physical card issuance
- Multi-currency card spending
- Instant payout to card
- ATM withdrawal support
- Expense management features

**Blockchain Settlement Network**
- Proprietary blockchain for instant settlements
- Reduced transaction fees
- Enhanced transparency and tracking
- Smart contract-based escrow
- Interoperability with major blockchains

---

## Future Initiatives

### Long-term Vision

These items are on our radar but not yet scheduled for a specific quarter:

- **Buy Now, Pay Later (BNPL)**: Flexible payment terms for B2B transactions
- **Open Banking Integration**: Direct bank account connections for collections
- **Tax Automation**: Automatic tax calculation and withholding for cross-border payments
- **Supply Chain Financing**: Invoice financing and working capital solutions
- **Loyalty & Rewards Program**: Cashback and points for high-volume users
- **Instant Settlements**: Real-time money movement for supported corridors
