# Virtual Accounts 2.0

**Released: September 20, 2025**

Major upgrade to our virtual accounts feature with enhanced multi-currency support and instant reconciliation.

## What's New

### 20+ Currency Support
Virtual accounts now available in USD, EUR, GBP, SGD, AUD, CAD, JPY, and more.

### Real-time Reconciliation
Automatic matching of incoming payments to collect records with instant notifications.

### QR Code Payments
Generate QR codes for easy payment collection via mobile banking apps.

### Enhanced Reporting
Detailed transaction reports with custom date ranges and export options.

### Webhook Enhancements
More granular event notifications for payment tracking and reconciliation.

## Benefits

- Faster payment collection
- Reduced manual reconciliation
- Better customer experience
- Lower operational costs
