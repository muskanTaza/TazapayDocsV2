## Q4 2025 - Connected Accounts Platform

**Released: December 15, 2025**

We're excited to announce the launch of our Connected Accounts platform, enabling marketplaces and platforms to seamlessly manage payouts on behalf of their sellers and service providers.

### Key Features

- **Sub-account Management**: Create and manage unlimited sub-accounts under your main account
- **Flexible Entity Types**: Support for both individual and business entities
- **Automated KYB**: Streamlined onboarding with automated document verification
- **Custom Workflows**: Configure approval flows and payout schedules per sub-account
- **Granular Permissions**: Role-based access control for team management

### Getting Started

Check out our [Connected Accounts Guide](/Guides/connected-accounts/overview) to learn how to integrate this powerful feature into your platform.

## Q3 2025 - Virtual Accounts 2.0

**Released: September 20, 2025**

Major upgrade to our virtual accounts feature with enhanced multi-currency support and instant reconciliation.

### What's New

- **20+ Currency Support**: Virtual accounts now available in USD, EUR, GBP, SGD, and more
- **Real-time Reconciliation**: Automatic matching of incoming payments to collect records
- **QR Code Payments**: Generate QR codes for easy payment collection
- **Enhanced Reporting**: Detailed transaction reports with custom date ranges
- **Webhook Enhancements**: More granular event notifications for payment tracking
