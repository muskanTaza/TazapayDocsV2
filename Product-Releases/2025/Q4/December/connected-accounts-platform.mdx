# Connected Accounts Platform

**Released: December 15, 2025**

We're excited to announce the launch of our Connected Accounts platform, enabling marketplaces and platforms to seamlessly manage payouts on behalf of their sellers and service providers.

## Key Features

### Sub-account Management
Create and manage unlimited sub-accounts under your main account, each with its own balance and transaction history.

### Flexible Entity Types
Support for both individual and business entities with customized onboarding flows.

### Automated KYB
Streamlined onboarding with automated document verification and compliance checks.

### Custom Workflows
Configure approval flows and payout schedules per sub-account to match your business requirements.

### Granular Permissions
Role-based access control for team management with customizable permission levels.

## Getting Started

Check out our [Connected Accounts Guide](/Guides/connected-accounts/overview) to learn how to integrate this powerful feature into your platform.

## Use Cases

- **Marketplaces**: Manage seller payouts automatically
- **Gig Platforms**: Handle freelancer payments at scale
- **SaaS Platforms**: Enable embedded payments for your users
