---
title: "Overview"
description: "A payment attempt is an attempt by your customer to make a payment using a particular . The payment attempt contains the customer specific information for a payment. For example, in case of a card payment, it is cardholder name, issuing bank, issuing country, etc."
---

Each checkout session can have multiple payment attempts. In case the previous payment attempt fails, the customer can go ahead using the same or a different payment method.
