# How It Works

* Users download the PayMaya app and register for an account, typically requiring a valid mobile number.
* They can then load funds into their PayMaya wallet through various channels such as online banking, over-the-counter at partner establishments (e.g., convenience stores, remittance centers), and through PayMaya agents.
* Once funded, users can make online and in-store purchases using their virtual or physical PayMaya card or by scanning QR codes with the PayMaya app.
* The platform also enables peer-to-peer transfers to other PayMaya users, bill payments for utilities and other services, mobile prepaid reloading, and even cash withdrawals at ATMs (using the physical card).
