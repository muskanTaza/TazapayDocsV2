---
title: "Overview"
description: "Funds can be transferred among accounts on the Tazapay network instantaneously and without any fee. These transfers can be performed using the unique deposit address associated with your account."
---

To receive payments, you can share your unique deposit address with the sender.

To make payments, you can ask the beneficiary for their deposit address.

You can fetch the deposit address by logging into the Tazapay dashboard and navigating to Settings.
