# How It Works

* Users activate their ShopeePay wallet within the Shopee app and can link their bank accounts, debit/credit cards, or top up their balance through online and over-the-counter channels.
* Primarily used for seamless and secure payments for purchases made within the Shopee app, often offering exclusive incentives.
* Increasingly accepted for offline payments at physical stores and merchants by scanning QR codes using the Shopee app.
* Facilitates other financial transactions such as sending money to other Shopee users, banks, and e-wallets, paying bills, and buying mobile load and data.

# Limits and Expiration

The following is the upper and lower limits for ShopeePay transactions:

* **Minimum Limit**: USD 0.5
* **Maximum Limit**: USD 850

The default expiration for a transaction is **30 minutes.**
