title: "Bank Accounts And Reconciliation"
---
You can add your bank details to receive settlement of your payments by logging on to the <a href="https://dashboard.tazapay.com" target="_blank">Tazapay Dashboard</a>.

Tazapay allows you to add local currency bank accounts as well as USD bank account. USD settlements, however, come at an additional cost.
